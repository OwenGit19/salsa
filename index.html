Welcome to Learn Salsa Dancing

Our website is designed to teach you everything you need to know to start salsa dancing. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced dancer, we have lessons that will help you improve your skills and master the dance.

Beginner Lessons

Our beginner lessons cover the basics of salsa dancing, including footwork, timing, and partnering techniques. These lessons are designed for people who have never danced salsa before or who are just starting out.

  • Lesson 1: Basic Steps
  • Lesson 2: Timing and Rhythm
  • Lesson 3: Partnering Techniques

Intermediate Lessons

Our intermediate lessons build on the basics covered in the beginner lessons and introduce more advanced techniques and patterns. These lessons are designed for people who have some experience with salsa dancing and want to take their skills to the next level.

  • Lesson 4: Turns and Spins
  • Lesson 5: Cross-Body Lead
  • Lesson 6: Shines
